# What is My Topic

Amazon Online Shopping

This project is to analyze data about online shopping on Amazon. Online shopping has developed rapidly in recent years as it becomes part of daily life for people. Compared with shopping in the store, online shopping provides various products in different catafories for people to choose and offers much more conviniece shopping experience with lower prices. Online shopping sales reach a new height every year that means there are more and more people tend to use online shopping to buy things they need.

What is more, because of Covid-19, people work online and hardly go outside for shopping in order to avoid crowd. Therefore, people choose to buy almost everything they need through online shopping websites or apps like Amazon because of the convenience, abundance, and safety.

It is interesting to think about the things bought online are good or suitable and how to choose products or compare the similiar product for different stores.
