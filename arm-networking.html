# Summary

Association Rule Mining (ARM) is a method of analysis that utilizes relational information to identify items that appear frequently together. Here, this analysis looks specifically at which words frequently appear together across hundreds of reviews. One can clearly see by the relationships described that there were several words that were commonly used for reviews. In the figures above, one can see that there are 4 main groups of words that commonly appear in reviews together.
